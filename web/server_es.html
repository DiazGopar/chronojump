Reliability on the server. Two levels:

1)General data Uploaded by anyone with the technology.
2)Reliable data Accredited by SAFE research group (Universitat Ramon Llull).
Reliable data. Profile Evaluator-trainer
  • National trainer.
  • Current work: club, institution, category.
  • Years as elite trainer.
  • Historical on trained sports and specialties.
  • Experience on using measurement equipment.
  • Career (100 words)
Reliable data. Profile Evaluator-researcher
  • Maximum reached degree.
  • Teacher at University?
  • Sport science researches using measure.
  • Other researches. Years as researcher.
  • University research groups.
  • Received grants.
  • Career (100 words)
Reliable data. Criteria by session: Informed consent - Warming up - Protocol variations (if any) - New tests protocols and validations (if any) - Data has been published? Where? - Justification of the research (100 words).
