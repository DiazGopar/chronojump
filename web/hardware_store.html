Chronopic 3

New Chronopic 3 already mounted with two firmware options (you can choose minimum detectable time):
  • 50ms: suitable for jumping on platforms (helps to avoid bogus jumps when jump technique is inadequate)
  • 10ms: suitable for running between photocells
You can change the firmware anytime.
Price: 43€.

Circuit board jump platform

High quality platform. Resistant to flexion (fiberglass) and with good conductivity. All circuit area produces contact. Oposite to old rods platforms, here there are no false contacts or problems produced by the toes being placed between rods at jumping or falling.

Sizes:
  • Din A2: 75€
  • Din A3: 55€
  • Din A4: 45€
  • (You can ask for other sizes)

Photocells

Velleman PEM10D Photocells. Features:
  • Response time (5-100 ms)
  • Adjustable sensibility
  • Input voltage AC-DC from 12 to 250V
  • Relay output (pluggable to Chronopic)
Price: 1 Photocell includes emitter and receptor, and costs 69€.

Typical uses

These are typical uses of Chronojump equipment. Download software to see all included tests and how to define new tests.
  • (A) Bosco jumps and other jumps
    1 Chronopic
    1 contact platform DIN-A2
  • (B,C) Runs
    1 Chronopic
    2 Photocells, or 1 contact platform DIN-A4 and 1 photocell.
We recommend to put photocells at the height of the head.
